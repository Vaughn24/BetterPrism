SHOWCASE

As Philanthropists, we engaged in helping people. Our goal is to able to extend help to those people who are in need of help.

Feeding Program

BetterPrism conducts feeding programs to various places from all over the world. We partner with the Local Government Units from different areas, and we feed thousands of people who need proper nutrition. Due to poverty, thousands of families couldn't provide appropriate nutritional foods to eat. Hence, we offer food supplies to places that need reasonable assistance.

Save the Planet

BetterPrism has created a program called "Save the Planet." This program is broad. Included in this program is the promotion of the utilization of recyclable things. Instead of using plastic bags, we promote the usage of eco-bags. BetterPrism is manufacturing eco-friendly bags and being donated to millions of people from all over the world.

Tree Planting Program

BetterPrism has resolved with various Environmental Agencies from all over the world to conduct tree-planting programs every month in different places. We also have a dedicated team to take care of the trees as they grow.

Our Partners

UNWFP

United Nations World Food Programme

The World Food Programme (WFP) is the leading humanitarian organization saving lives and changing lives, delivering food assistance in emergencies and working with communities to improve nutrition and build resilience.

UNEP

United Nations Environmental Programme

The United Nations Environment Programme (UNEP) is the leading global environmental authority that sets the global environmental agenda, promotes the coherent implementation of the environmental dimension of sustainable development within the United Nations system, and serves as an authoritative advocate for the global environment.

TNC

The Nature Conservancy

The Nature Conservancy is a global environmental nonprofit working to create a world where people and nature can thrive. Founded in the U.S. through grassroots action in 1951, The Nature Conservancy has grown to become one of the most effective and wide-reaching environmental organizations in the world. Thanks to more than a million members and the dedicated efforts of our diverse staff and over 400 scientists, we impact conservation in 72 countries and territories: 38 by direct conservation impact and 34 through partners.

About Us


Mission

BetterPrism is an organization that helps to fight the injustice of poverty. Develop environmental best practices in programs, products and services. And help people build better futures for themselves, hold the powerful accountable, and save lives in disasters.


Vision

We believe that people have equal intrinsic value regardless of the circumstances of their birth, and that animals’ lives have value, too. We believe economic development and technological innovation have greatly increased human well-being. We’re optimistic that this trend will continue, and we hope to play a part in accelerating it.


Core Values

Exceptional Service – We nurture lifelong relationships with our clients that span for generations.

Trustworthiness – We preserve donor intent and hold ourselves accountable to those who place their trust in us.

Leadership – We lead and collaborate with others to build a better community.

Passion – We inspire passion by guiding a donor’s philanthropic journey and encourage charitable giving throughout our community.
